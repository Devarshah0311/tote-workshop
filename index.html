— About the Workshop —

Bring your creative side out.

Spend a slow, sunny Sunday with paints, brushes and a blank canvas tote. No experience needed — just an open mind and a good playlist in your ears. You leave with a bag you actually made, and a room full of new friends.

🎨

All materials provided

Canvas totes, fabric paints, brushes, palettes, stencils. Just show up.

☕

Refreshments included

Coffee, iced teas, and small bites while you paint. On the house.

🌿

Guided, not gate-kept

Templates and inspiration boards if you want them. Total freedom if you don't.

🫶

Come solo or paired

Great date idea, better girls'-day. Also perfect for a quiet solo reset.

— About Us —

Small studio, big canvas energy.

notyour.regularsaga is a tiny creative studio that runs slow, hands-on workshops for people who want to make more and scroll less. We started with a spare table, a few brushes, and the belief that everyone is more creative than they think.

"We don't teach art. We hold space for it."
